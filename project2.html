Folding Table

This project focuses on the design and creation of a folding table that is space-efficient, portable, and practical. It utilizes a simple but sturdy mechanism for quick folding and unfolding, making it ideal for small spaces and multi-purpose use.

Key Features

  • Quick-fold mechanism for easy setup and storage
  • Lightweight yet durable construction for portability
  • Space-saving design suitable for apartments and small offices
  • Affordable and simple to manufacture
  • Versatile usage for temporary setups and events

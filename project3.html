Plant Growth Monitoring System

This project involves developing a simple yet effective system to monitor and optimize the growth of plants by tracking environmental factors like moisture, light, and temperature. The system provides valuable insights into plant needs, allowing for better management and higher crop yields.

Key Features

  • Real-time monitoring of soil moisture, light intensity, and temperature
  • User-friendly interface for data visualization
  • Automated alerts for optimal plant care
  • Affordable and easy to set up
  • Water and resource conservation through precise monitoring
